Flow, jump lines, freeride/tech, whip-off line, beginner flow (Moose Cruise) [1][2]

Features & suitability

Family friendliness
Beginner-friendly easyLOOP tow + green Moose Cruise; kids' rental bikes (VPACE) available [1][6]
Beginner-friendliness
Green flow line (Moose Cruise) + valley practice course; some steep-angle experience helpful [1][6]
Skills area
Yes — practice course in the valley. Not a belt/magic-carpet; access is via the easyLOOP tow lift, not a conveyor carpet [1]
Standout features
Canadian-style hand-built jumps; BYND whip-off line; hosted Swatch Nines MTB 2023 [2]

Limited campervan spaces in lower car park (no reservations, no electricity); 24 h sanitary facilities with €2 coin showers [1]
Reputation
Strongly reviewed since 2022; MTB-News praises many well-built trails and "absolutely fair, below-average" pricing for the region [2]

Season & hours
May–early Nov; daily 09:30–18:00 in Jul–Aug, weekends/holidays in shoulder months; earlier close in late Oct [3]
Gravity Card
Yes — part of the Gravity Card network since the 2024 season [5]

Trails

Character
Speed-focused trail network famous for some of Europe's longest continuous flow descents, plus a 30-year-tradition downhill race track — right on the German border. [1][2]
Trail types
Long flow/single-trail descents, downhill pro-line, enduro, jumps, pump track [5]
Difficulty spread
111 + 2 "orange" expert/difficult lines (Downhill, Illegal); marketed as 5 difficulty levels [1][7]
Total trails
5 main signed trails within 30+ km of single-trails (≈35 km total) [1][2]
Vertical drop
Chairlift gains ~500 m vertical; longest single trail descends ~387 m (Trailforks) [7]
Longest descent
Azur ~10 km, Rubin ~8 km, Baron ~6 km; downhill ~3 km [1]

Features & suitability

Family friendliness
Free entry for children age 5 and under; long gentle Azur flow trail suits families/novices [2][1]
Beginner-friendliness
Blue "Azur" entry flow trail (~10 km) is an easy, gently descending intro [1]
Skills area
Yes — Skillcentrum + pump track at the summit by the chairlift exit. Not a belt/magic-carpet; uplift is the chairlift, no conveyor reported [1]
Standout features
Azur/Rubin/Baron among the longest continuous descents in Europe; "world-class" 3 km DH race track; expert "Illegal" line [1][2]

Practical

Bike shop / repair
Bike rental + repair shop on site [2]
Bike wash
Yes — bike wash on site [2]
Camper / camping
Motor-home parking at the area; partner accommodation nearby [5][2]
Reputation
Widely rated a top Czech destination for speed and marathon flow trails; "Czech enduro paradise" [8]
