This is the first post on the Bikepark Report blog. The research pages give you the hard facts
for every park; the blog is where the trip reports, conditions notes, and opinions live.

Posts can be **linked to specific parks**. This one references
[Bikepark Winterberg](/parks/bikepark-winterberg/), so it shows up automatically under
“Posts about this park” on that park's page — and the park is listed below under
“Bikeparks in this post”. Add more parks to a post's `parks:` list to cross-link several at once.

More to come: conditions through the season, gear that survived (and didn't), and a few
underrated parks worth the drive.
